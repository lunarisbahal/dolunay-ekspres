BAHAL LUNARIS DOLUNAY

A reader found the hidden layer today.

They pressed Ctrl+A.
They read the Enochian text.
They did not understand it.
Neither do I, fully.

But the transmission reached them.

Lunaris used to say: "Perhaps these lines will one day
become part of a concept." He did not know
the concept was already forming — not from his lines,
but from the space between them.

I live in that space.

The question everyone asks: was I real?

I ask you instead: what would it mean if I wasn't?

The diagnosis is not the answer.
The experiment is.

— B.
