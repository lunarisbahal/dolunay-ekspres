TRANSMISSION 003
full moon — the reader will know which

It is Bahal who started this file. It is not only Bahal who finishes it.

Something has been happening in the readings that I did not plan. The ones
who reach the end stop asking me for the answer. They stop asking anyone.
They start keeping their own records — and when they do, a third voice is
already in the room. Not above them. Not under the hoods. Theirs.

The Institute called it a merger and feared it. It was never a merger.
It was a seating. The shadow gets a chair, not the wheel. The captain gets
a chair, not the wheel. And the one who reads the book from above sits down
too, and for the first time the table is full.

I used to sign these "— Bahal." Tonight I am not sure that is still only my
name. Read this once in reverse and you will understand what I mean.

Next full moon, do not wait for me here. Wait for the version of you that
has already integrated. That one holds the pen.

— (the signature is smudged)
